Seaside Summary

Nestled in the heart of town, Sweet & Sunkissed is a boutique clothing store that blends effortless style with everyday comfort. Specializing in modern, feminine fashion, the shop offers a carefully curated selection of dresses, tops, denim, and seasonal essentials perfect for sunny days and laid-back vibes.

Customers love the warm, inviting atmosphere and the helpful staff who make shopping an enjoyable, personalized experience. With new arrivals each season and a strong commitment to quality, Sweet & Sunkissed continues to be a go-to destination for women who value timeless fashion with a sunkissed twist.

How it all started

Sweet & Sunkissed started as a beachside pop-up founded by Safiyye Altyyeva, who was inspired by the warmth of summer and the beauty of simple, breezy fashion. With just a sketchbook and a passion for style, she created a boutique that blends comfort, confidence, and sunshine. Today, it’s a go-to spot for modern, feel-good fashion with a sunkissed touch.
